Stir Fried Lamb

Served over white rice

Ingredients

  • Some lamb fillet cut into thin pieces.
  • 2 cloves of garlic, grated
  • Ginger the size of one clove of garlic, grated
  • Some veggies: Carrot, yellow pepper and sugar snaps
  • Olive oil
  • Sugar snaps
  • 1 piece yellow pepper
  • 2 pieces of carrot
  • 3 tbsp. soy sauce
  • 2 tsp. tamarind paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Dhania

Instructions

  1. Prepare the veggies
  2. Add a bit of olive oil in a pan, the meat, the ginger, and the garlic. Stir on medium heat. Do not allow the garlic and ginger to burn
  3. Add 3 tbsp. of soy sauce and 2 tsp. of tamarind paste.
  4. Keep stirring frequently. Add just a little bit of water if you think it is drying out too much. Add salt to taste
  5. Once the lamb is cooked, add the carrots. Wait for 5 minutes then add the sugar snaps and the yellow pepper.
  6. Then taste for salt, add if it needs any and chop up a little bit of dhania to fold in. Fold the dhania into the stir fry and you are done
  7. Serve immediately over steamed rice
